// About

A guide for
this terrain.

Hayden Trowbridge came to know God through a screen. Sermon after sermon, learning to pray, learning apologetics, watching pastors through a glowing rectangle. He is also among the first generation to grow up on social media. He has lived inside these digital environments since before he could name what they were doing to him. He knows deeply how they shape our desires.

After three years working alongside one of the world's largest YouTubers, Hayden founded Beautiful Internet to bring those strategies to a redemptive purpose. Not a consulting firm. A formation ministry that uses digital strategy to help churches and Christian organizations draw people toward embodied life with God.
